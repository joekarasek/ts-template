Meet the Team

Jeffrey G. Priluck, DMD, MAGD, FAAID

Dr. Priluck received a B.A. in biology from Boston University. He attended the University of Pennsylvania School of Dental Medicine in Philadelphia where he earned his Doctor of Dental Medicine degree in 1973. He received post graduate training in a teaching Fellowship at Emory University School of Dentistry in Atlanta. During this time, he served as a clinical instructor in the Department of Operative Dentistry at Emory. In 1978 Dr. Priluck opened his private practice in its present Dunwoody location. Dr. Nordone joined him in practice in 1987.

Dr. Priluck received his Mastership of the Academy of General Dentistry after an intensive five year curriculum involving all phases of dentistry and over 1100 hours of post graduate study. In 2012 he achieved credentialing as a Fellow in the American Academy of Implant Dentistry, an honor that is awarded to dentists that have practiced implant dentistry for at least five years and completed an additional 100 hours of continuing education in dental implant placement. He serves as the Immediate Past President of the Georgia Academy of General Dentistry and has been honored by Governor Sonny Perdue.

His postgraduate education also includes the world famous Pankey Institute. He is a graduate of the world renowned Las Vegas Institute for Advanced Cosmetic Dental Studies as well as the Rosenthal Institute of Cosmetic Dentistry at the New York University School of Dentistry.

Dr. Priluck is married and has two children. He and his wife are founding members of various religious and civic organizations including the Dunwoody Evening Rotary Club.

Albert P. Nordone, DDS

Dr. Nordone graduated from Loyola College in Maryland in 1982 with a dual degree in Biology and French. After research at the University of Paris, France, and Johns Hopkins University he attended Emory University School of Dentistry where he received his D.D.S. degree in 1986.

After practicing dentistry as an associate in Buckhead until October 1987, Dr. Nordone joined Dr. Priluck in his practice in Dunwoody. He became a partner in September of 1989.

Dr. Nordone is a member of the American Dental Association, Georgia Dental Association, Northern District Dental Society, Hinman Dental Society and the American Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry. He served as Chairman of several committees in these organizations and sat on the Executive Council of the Northern District Dental Society for 3 years. His postgraduate education has included completion of all six continuums at the Pankey Institute in Florida.

Dr. Nordone is a graduate of the world-renowned Las Vegas Institute for Advanced Dental Studies. This institute provides rigorous postgraduate training in advanced cosmetic techniques.

Dr. Nordone is married with 4 children. He enjoys time with his family and participating in his children's activities. He and his wife, Daphne, are very active in their church and vigorously support several charitable organizations.

Our Services

By actively preventing disease and decay through regular home care, professional dental cleanings and regular exams, we'll partner with you to maintain your healthy, beautiful smile. Prevention is always better than treatment, so if it's been a while since you've seen a dentist, please give us a call or click to request an appointment.

Smile Makeovers

Cosmetic dentistry is one of the most cost-effective ways to improve your appearance. A beautiful, bright, healthy smile will increase self-confidence and overall life satisfaction. Cosmetic dentistry includes things like tooth-colored restorations, dental implants, and crowns. We have many options, so please call us today!

Smile-Whitening

Many everyday things like coffee, tea and wine can significantly dull teeth. Smile-whitening enables us to significantly brighten your smile for that dazzling white smile you deserve.

Family Care

From toddlers to the elderly, our skilled dental team is familiar with the unique needs of every patient, no matter their age. Our goal is to help them develop great oral habits that will last them a lifetime. Join us today and discover how our practice can help treat your entire family.

Digital X-Rays

Our use of digital x-ray technology reduces your radiation exposure and eliminates the use of chemicals. It’s better for you and the environment.

It's also easy for you to see what the doctor sees. Your digital x-ray can be seen on a computer screen located next to your dental chair. This helps you understand your needs and make informed choices about your treatment.

Implant Dentistry

Dental implants are a great option for replacing missing teeth, improving chewing ability, eliminating bridges, and replacing dentures. Several implants may provide a base for a series of artificial teeth known as a fixed bridge. Implants can even be used to secure a full set of removable dentures for people who have no remaining natural teeth.

Crowns and Bridges

Protect and keep badly decayed or fractured teeth while maintaining a beautiful smile. Made to perfectly match the shape and shade of your other teeth, they're durable, strong, and undetectable.

Crowns should be placed before the tooth is so decayed that it may fracture. This can often help prevent the expense of root canal therapy in the future. If a tooth is too damaged to have a crown applied, a porcelain bridge may be a good alternative.

Cosmetic Dentures

A well made denture is not only comfortable and functions properly; it also allows the wearer to smile with confidence, knowing that their smile is no different than it would be with natural teeth.

Veneers

Veneers are very thin porcelain pieces that are custom made to adhere directly to the front of teeth without grinding or shaving. Transform your smile without drilling on your natural teeth!

In the hands of a highly skilled dentist, porcelain laminates can provide you with an incredibly natural, beautiful smile. They are virtually undetectable and can dramatically improve your appearance. They are durable and long-lasting when compared to other cosmetic treatments.

Implant-Supported Dentures

Do you wear dentures or have a mouthful of missing or failing teeth? Eat what you want again, smile, laugh, and look and feel years younger with All-on-4® implant-supported dentures.

Sedation Dentistry

Sedation dentistry is an excellent treatment for patients with extensive dental needs, or who are concerned about anxiety, difficulty getting numb, or sensitive teeth.
